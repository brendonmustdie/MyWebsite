Week 12:

Critical Analysis of UI and UX:

Landing Page

My landing page is fairly welcoming, and the "back button" is very noticeable - and obvious. There are some issues with it - the page is scrollable but theres no indication of it. In my next iteration I'll include a nav bar on the left hand side, and maybe an animation that allows users to see they can scroll down. Maybe some bouncing arrows.

I do find it be lacking and boring, maybe in my next iteration I'll include an about me section.

Gallery Section

I'm pretty happy with my gallery section, I think the overlays are great and I think they convey what I wanted them too. The section doesn't have a heading though, which makes it hard to know what it is. I also don't think I indicated very well that you could click on it. Otherwise, I'm pretty happy with it.

Blog Section

I'm the most happy with this section. The lack of headings means it's not very clear, but I think the icons and the overlay make it easier to know what's going on. I'm pretty chuffed with the colour palette.
