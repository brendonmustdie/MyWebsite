Design Page

The first thing that's noticeably different about my website is the colour palette - I've done a complete 180 from the soft blues of the previous website. The browns convey a calmer and more professional than the baby blues from the previous one. I like this font better as well - I feel like the new design really represents me a lot better than the previous one.
The image header was chosen because it conveys a more professional-student vibe.

The circle were chosen because I thought they would make users more likely to click to the new pages. The pictures I chose are meant to emphasis a social and down-to-earth version of myself and my website. But the goofy selfie is a nice break from the professionalism, and an introduction to me. Which what's really important about this website at all.
